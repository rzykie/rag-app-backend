Company Handbook
================

Welcome to our company! This handbook contains important information about our company policies and procedures.

Leave Policy
------------
- Employees are entitled to 20 paid vacation days per year.
- Sick leave is 10 days per year.
- Parental leave is 12 weeks.

Events
------
- The annual company retreat is in December.
- We have monthly team lunches.

Contributions
-------------
- We encourage contributions to open-source projects.
- The company matches 401k contributions up to 5%.
